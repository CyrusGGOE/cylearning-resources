Welcome to integrated science of Grade 8!

We will be dealing with 3 major topics:
1. **Mixtures, Elements and Compounds:** This is the Chemistry part. We will be looking at elements and compounds, their attributes and changes.
2. **Living things and their environment:** Basically Biology. We will be looking at cells and their behaviors.
3. **Force and energy:** We will be looking at energy. We will also be looking at how this energy can be transformed. We will also be looking at pressure as a SI unit and how to calculate pressure.
