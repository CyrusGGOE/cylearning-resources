# Human Origin
## Stories of Human Origin
### A. Traditional stories of human origin from African communities
Here are a few examples of traditional stories:
**1. Agikuyu community:**
- They believe that Ngai, the creator, lived on Mount Kenya.
- Ngai made Gikuyu, the first man, and Mumbi, his wife.
- They lived at Mukurwe Wa Nyagathanga near present day Muranga.
- Gikuyu and Mumbi made 9 daughters, and later became the 9 clans.
**2. Bukusu community:**
- The Bukusu believe in Wele Khakaba (Meaning God the creator).
- Wele Khakaba created Mwambu, the first man through mud at a place called Mumbo (Meaning west).
- Wele Khakaba then made Sela, the wife to Mwambu. Together they made the Bukusu community.
**3. Maasai community:**
- The Maasai believe in a God called Enkai.
- Enkai created the first man and gave him cattle, which are considered sacred.
- Enkai lowered the cattle using a long rope.
