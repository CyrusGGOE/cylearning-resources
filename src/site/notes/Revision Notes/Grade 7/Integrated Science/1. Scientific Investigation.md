# Introduction to Integrated Science

## What is the meaning of integrated science?
- Integrated science is the study of how a learner understands the natural world through:
    - The study of composition of matter (Chemistry)
    - The study of living things (Biology)
    - The study of the relationship between matter and energy (Physics)
## What are the components of Integrated Science?
1. **Chemistry:** The study of the structure, composition, properties and changes of matter.
    - The main point is: Chemistry is the study of the composition of matter.
2. **Biology:** The study of living things.
3. **Physics:** The study of the relationship between matter and energy.
