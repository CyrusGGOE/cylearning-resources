Pre - technical studies is a subject that integrates three components into one.

# Components of Pre - technical Studies
**1. Technical studies:**
- This is the component that involves acquiring technical and vocational skills.
**2. Computer science:**
- This is the component that involves learning how computers work and how computers can be used to fix problems.
**3. Business studies:**
- The study of activities that involve production, distribution and consumption of goods and services.
# Roles of Pre - technical studies in day - to - day life

1. Helps learners to develop practical and technical skills.
2. It offers a wide range of careers like mechanics and software engineers.
3. It helps learners to observe safety in their environment.
4. It promotes independence, self-learning and self-resilience by building confidence and being able to do tasks independently.
5. It promotes problem solving skills in fixing common problems in the environment.
6. It enhances collaboration and effective communication.

# Examples of Careers in Pre - technical studies

Here are a few examples of careers:
- A mechanic
- A software engineer
- A carpenter
- An architect
- An electrician
- A teacher
- Engineers like civil engineers, mechanical engineers e.t.c.
